couples corner 237

Find the love of your life on #CouplesCorner237.
Couple’s corner 237, is a digital and TV reality show where 20 random contestants from the ten
regions of Cameroon and Nigeria are kept in a building under surveillance to monitor and
study their interactions.Their performances and actions are also aired live, online and on TV.
These housemates are rewarded or evicted based on their performances until two
contestants are declared the “Couple Corner 237 winner”.
The eventual winning couple goes home with a cash prices to enable them start up their life.
